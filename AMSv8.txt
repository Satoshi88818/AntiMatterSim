AntimatterSim v8 — Level 8 Full Codebase

Upgrade from v7 (Level 7) to v8 (Level 8): Clinical and Predictive Intelligence

This release transitions AntimatterSim from a Scientific Simulator to a Clinical Research Platform. The five pillars of Level 8 are:

Nuclear Recoil & Heavy Fragment Transport — full trajectory and secondary interaction tracking for spallation products

Secondary Activation & PET Modeling — production and decay of radio-isotopes (¹¹C, ¹⁵O) for in-vivo dosimetry verification

DICOM Clinical Integration — CT-to-geometry conversion via Hounsfield Unit → material mapping on a voxel-by-voxel basis

AI-Driven Surrogate Modeling (Neural Monte Carlo) — 3D CNN surrogate trained on AMSv7/v8 simulations for real-time dose prediction

Radiobiological Outcome Modeling — Tumor Control Probability (TCP) and Normal Tissue Complication Probability (NTCP) for clinical decision support

What Changed vs v7

Pillarv7 (Level 7)v8 (Level 8)Heavy RecoilLocal energy deposit onlyFull Fragment Transport loop: trajectory + secondary interactions + low-dose envelopeNuclear ActivationNot modeledPET Modeling: ¹¹C / ¹⁵O production, positron decay, annihilation gamma predictionClinical GeometryGDML / STL / CSG phantomsDICOM CT-to-Geometry: Hounsfield Units → material + density, voxel-by-voxelDose SpeedMonte Carlo only (~hours)Neural Monte Carlo surrogate: 3D CNN predicts dose in milliseconds (≥95% fidelity)RadiobiologyRBE from microdosimetryFull TCP / NTCP clinical outcome models; plan comparison & decision support
